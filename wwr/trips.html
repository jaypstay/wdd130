Gauley Blast Class IV-V (Advanced/Expert) This adrenaline-pumping trip is not for the faint of heart. Navigate the legendary Gauley River, known for its challenging rapids with names like "Lost and Found," "Housekeeper," and "Heaven Can Wait." This is a full-day trip with experienced guides who will lead you through technical maneuvers and ensure your safety. Be prepared for an exhilarating ride with breathtaking scenery. Summer & Fall
Family Fun on the Lower Klamath Difficulty This scenic trip along the Klamath River is perfect for families with children aged 8 and above. Paddle through gentle rapids with exciting splashes, interspersed with calm stretches ideal for swimming and enjoying the beautiful river environment. Guides will provide instruction and ensure everyone has a safe and enjoyable experience. The trip might also include educational elements about the local flora and fauna. Spring & Summer
Moab Majesty Class I-II (Relaxed/Scenic) Embark on a peaceful journey down the Colorado River as the sun dips below the horizon, painting the Moab landscape in warm hues. This tranquil trip is ideal for nature lovers and those seeking a relaxing experience. Enjoy stunning canyon views, keep an eye out for wildlife, and learn about the history and geology of the area from your knowledgeable guide. Fall through Spring
